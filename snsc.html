Surfers Not Street Children

20% of our profits are donated to Surfers Not Street Children, an incredible charity that empowers street children through the transformative power of surfing and mentorship. Their holistic approach provides not just surf training, but also emotional support, education, and guidance, helping vulnerable children overcome their circumstances and build brighter futures. Check out their instagram if you would like to learn more!
